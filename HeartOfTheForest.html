Heart of the Forest


Project Type: University Group Project
Primary Role: Generalist Designer
Software used: Unity, Maya, Substance Painter, Photoshop
Language Used: C#

  • Heart of the Forest is a project I was part of as a generalist game designer during for my 2nd year at Bournemouth University. The project consisted of 5 team members:

    - Producer

    - Lead programmer

    - Generalist Programmer

    - Lead Designer

    - Generalist Designer <--- me

  • Heart of the Forest is a 3D action wave defence style game, with a focus on the player instead of a defence system. Heart of the Forest will take the genre a step beyond the basic upgrade and watch style of gameplay.

    The main game mechanic features a day and night cycle where a time-gated peace period is enabled during the day, allowing the player to purchase upgrades. During the night cycle, a wave of enemies spawn from within the forest surrounding the village, in which the player is tasked with to defend.

    Various enemy types target specific targets. Enemies such as Bats, would only target the buildings, whereas the skeletons can target the buildings, core and the player if they are nearby. A rare specific enemy such as a giant skeleton, would ignore all targets and go straight for the core.

  • My Role

    I developed drafts and concept art for the environment, level and the general layout of the playable game area. When these concepts were agreed upon by the lead designer and producer. I started to develop 3D Models using Maya and Substance Painter. Due to the nature of this project I decided to create models with a more simplistic and low-poly look.
