About Me

Hello, my name is Deniss Kirillovs, a games programmer and designer. I graduated from Bournemouth University where I studied Games Design.

I've built this page for you to see some of my projects that I have developed over time. I strive for challenge and love discovering new technologies and techniques that help me create the world and vision we see in games today. My time at Bournemouth University has laid me a solid foundation for me to construct, refine and explore new and existing skills within the industry.
